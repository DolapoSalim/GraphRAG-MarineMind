About the Project
🌊
Domain: Marine Ecology
Focuses on interpreting imaging-based ecological variables — seagrass extent, benthic habitat, biodiversity — from Sentinel-2 satellite data and environmental monitoring records.
Active Research Prototype
🧠
Design: Anti-Hallucination RAG
All answers are grounded through two structured retrieval tiers before the LLM generates text. Every claim is traceable to a specific graph relation or statistical record — no confabulation.
Graph + Stats + LLM fusion
🔭
Roadmap: Vector + FastAPI
Planned Tier 3 adds vector-based document retrieval over scientific literature. FastAPI deployment and integration with real Sentinel-2 datasets from Copernicus Open Access Hub are on the roadmap.
